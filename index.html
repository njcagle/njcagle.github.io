About Me

My name is Nick Cagle, I am from a town called Faribault (pronounced: "Faribo") in Minnesota. This is my personal website that I created for myself to showcase my skills and experience in web development As of right now, my most refined skills are in HTML & CSS, as I have been learning and using them since my senior year of highschool. If you're on a computer, you can even test this out by shrinking the browser window to as thin as it can go. If you're on a tablet or smartphone, you're most likely seeing the shrinked version of my website. Still looks nice, huh?
